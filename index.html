KATHARSIS

Katharsis/Catharsis is to release your repressed emotions and experiences. Here is a place to share the hardest thing you ever had to go through, how you were feeling about it at that time, and how you dealt/are dealing with it. It will be not only a release of your emotions but also a hint for those who have the same situation.

【 rule 】
1. Do not share anything that others can identify individuals.
2. You don't need to share your name.
3. 400 characters maximum.
